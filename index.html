Luwuk Pride typical
Onyop

Onyop is a typical Central Sulawesi food made from sago and usually served with fish in Segara sauce (Banggai Regency) or tamarind sauce (Luwuk). Onyop is also commonly said to be similar to Papeda and Jenangan from East Java, which are both traditional culinary delights from Sago. However, the difference is, if Papeda is served as a staple food, then Onyop is served as a side dish.

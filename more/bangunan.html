Kilo 5 Luwuk Tourist Spot, Built on Two Floors with a Replica of the Maleo Bird

A total of three tourist destination spots have become icons of Luwuk City, Banggai Regency, Central Sulawesi. Namely, the Alun Alun Bumi Mutiara Luwuk spot, Teluk Lalong and the Kilo Lima Beach destination are included in the planning radar of Banggai Regent Amirudin Tamoreka. Of the three spots, this year only the Kilo Lima tourist destination, South Luwuk District, has its development prioritized by the Regional Government of Banggai Regency. "In accordance with the Banggai Regent's direction, the construction of the Kilo 5 Beach DTW (Tourist Attraction) is prioritized with a budget of IDR 15 billion," said Banggai Regency PUPR Functional Planning Officer Triansyah to the Luwuk Portal, Thursday (12/01/23). He said that the Kilo 5 tourist development was made of two floors, using local wisdom construction. Where on the roof you can see a replica of the maleo bird. "Hopefully this year we will have a 5 kilo tourist destination with modern construction with local characteristics," he said.
